overview

ARTeeth is an aesthetic dentistry Augmented Reality APP that allows users to view the after-effect of dental treatments. It provides picture as well as AR effects of two teeth treatments - teeth whitening and 'virtual smile'.

Corporating with dentists in Taiwan, this project will be adopted by clinics to bridge the gap between the aesthetic perception of dentists and their clients by making the results visible in real-time camera.
